Tracking overview

One place to see how you're doing and jump straight into each tool. Your own lead numbers are below; the buttons open Clarity, Google Analytics, and Meta in a new tab (you'll sign in to each the first time).

Quick guide to what each tool tells you:
• Your lead funnel — the people who actually gave you their details and how many went on toward a trial. This is your data.
• Microsoft Clarity — watch recordings of real visits and see heatmaps of where people click and how far they scroll. Best for "why did they leave?"
• Google Analytics — the numbers: how many visitors, where they came from (Google, social, email), and which pages they viewed. Best for "how much and from where?"
• Meta — activity from your Facebook/Instagram pixel and any ad-driven leads. Best if you run Meta ads.
